How To Use It

1. Circuit Design: CircuitLab provides a user-friendly interface for designing electronic circuits. You can drag and drop various components onto the canvas to create your circuit.2. Component Library: CircuitLab offers a comprehensive library of electronic components, including resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, transistors, op-amps, and many more. You can choose from these components to build your circuit.3. Simulation: One of the key features of CircuitLab is its circuit simulation capabilities. Once you design your circuit, you can simulate its behavior to analyze how it will function under different conditions.4. DC and AC Analysis: You can perform DC analysis to study the behavior of the circuit under steady-state conditions. AC analysis allows you to investigate how the circuit responds to varying frequencies.5. Transient Analysis: CircuitLab enables you to perform transient analysis to observe the circuit's behavior over time, especially when there are changes in input signals.6. Parameter Sweeps: You can perform parameter sweeps to study the effect of changing component values on the circuit's performance.7. Interactive Plotting: CircuitLab provides interactive plotting tools to visualize circuit waveforms, current, voltage, and other important parameters.8. Wavedrom Support: You can create timing diagrams using the Wavedrom editor to analyze digital circuit behavior.9. Collaboration and Sharing: You can share your circuits with others or collaborate with fellow users by sending links to your designs.
